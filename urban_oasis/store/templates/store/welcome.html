Welcome to Urban Oasis Furnishings

Urban Oasis furnishings is a newly established furniture retail store focusing on modern and trendy furniture for the stylish individual. It is located in Mukono, Uganda.
Urban Oasis furnishings is owned by Edward Masembe a Scientist and entrepreneur with keen eyes for quality craftsmanship and minimalist design.
Edward has recognized that while there are a handful of quality furniture stores, there are not very many quality modern furniture stores that offer unique designs and furnishings for reasonable prices.

Urban Oasis furnishings will carry quality pieces of modern-designed furniture that are moderately priced and will last for years. Whatever room you are outfitting, Urban Oasis furnishings will have something for you.

What We Do

We offer a wide range of furniture, from modern sofas to bespoke dining tables. Our team of craftsmen works with the finest materials to ensure that every piece is a masterpiece.
